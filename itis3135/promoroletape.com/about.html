Meet the founder

Rahiem Thompson is the founder and CEO. Rahiem uses creativity, passion and professionalism to catapult businesses to greater heights through video. He's a Thurgood Marshall scholar that received his bachelors of computer science at North Carolina A&T. Rahiem is also the founder of Pocket Producer™, a free online resource to hire creative professionals in your city. Graphic designers, to photographers, and everything in between, Pocket Producer™ is your one stop resource for all your creative needs.

Our Mission

At Roletape, our number one mission is to create one of a kind, high quality works of art for our clients. We started this company so that creative individuals can turn their talents into marketable skills and make a living doing what they love. What's better than that?

Our Vision

We have a clear vision to create a world where the creative independent has the resources to fully make a living based on their artistry. Our vision reflects all of our important values: integrity, service, excellence and of course, teamwork.
