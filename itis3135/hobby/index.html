What is a Onewheel?

Onewheel is an U.S based company that sells personal electric vehicles (PEVs). The Onewheel is an electric skateboard type vehicle that uses a rubber go-kart tire instead of skateboard wheels. There is only one wheel on the board and that is where it gets its name from. The tire is air filled like a normal car tire so this allows riders to go through a lot more variations of terrain in comparison to a normal electric skateboard. As of 2023, Onewheel has 4 models of Onewheels that they sell: the Onewheel Pint, the Onewheel Pint x, the Onewheel GT, and the Onewheel GT-S.

The difference between the models is range and speed. The Onewheel pint can go 16 mph with a range of 6-8 miles and the Onewheel GT can go 20 mph with a range of 20-32 miles. There is also the Onewheel GT-S model that can go 25 mph but has a lower range of 16-25 miles. All 4 models of the board are assembled in California, USA. The founder and CEO Kyle Doerksen intentionally kept production of the Onewheel in the U.S because he believes in giving the people here at home a stable job instead of exporting the labor to China. With this in mind, the "Made in USA" stamp of approval does come with an increased price tag.

Here are the price tags of each model as of 2023:

Onewheel Pint: $1,050 USD

Onewheel Pint x: $1,500 USD

Onewheel GT: $2,300 USD

Onewheel GT-S: $3,200 USD
