About Me

Hey! My name is Ramone and ever since I was a little kid, I was OBSESSED with computers and how they work. I mostly liked computers because I could play videogames on them, but as I grew older, I came to understand computers can be used for more than just videogames. My love for coding began in middle school when I was introduced to computer programming on a website called code.org. In my free time between assignments in middle school, I would complete some of the puzzles on code.org for fun! My teacher told me that computer programming in the real world is similar to some of the exercises on code.org and this made me so happy because I was already pretty proficient at completing the puzzles!

From that moment on, I knew I wanted to become a computer programmer. As I progressed in my academic career and learned more about the different job fields that encompass a "computer programmer", I settled on becoming a website developer. I'm interested in becoming a website developer because of the origins of my love for computer programming. code.org got me really used to the code I write being linked to a graphical element of an interface. I feel it's easier for me to understand what my code is doing when I can visualize it.
